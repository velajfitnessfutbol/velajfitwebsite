Our Story

Velaj Fitness and Futbol was created by two sisters who wanted to share their passion and love for soccer and fitness with their community. VFF founders Linda Velaj is a former NCAA D2 All-American at the University of Bridgeport and sister Furtuna Velaj is a former NCAA D1 All-New England at Quinnipiac University, Albanian national team player and professional soccer player. The two sisters started VFF to offer more individualized soccer training and general fitness for youth soccer players and adults looking to get fit. No matter your fitness level or soccer training we have a personalized program for you, from 1 on 1 training to small classes. Trust us to help you become a better, fitter, and healthier YOU!
